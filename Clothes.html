Her Style

The Queen always has a beautiful outfit for almost every occasion. she gets her colorful ensembles of clothing custom by designer Angela Kelly. She usually pairs her clothing with her signature black Launer handbag.
For her shoes she usually wears Anello & Davide loafers and on a sunny day she wears a beautiful boater hat to protect her from the sun. Even though the queens clothes are amazing, were sure that she would look just as beautiful in the clothes of a poor woman.
